About this game
The game has 3 levels of difficulty.
Click the squares to move them. You can only move a square which has an empty square as a neighbour and they will switch place. Place all the squares in the right order to match the image and you win.
The register is optional. If you do register, you will have a timer and your score will be displayed in a table.
